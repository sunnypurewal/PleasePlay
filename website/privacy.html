No Personal Data Collected

Sonnio Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

What We Collect

Sonnio does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There is no account creation, no tracking, and no third-party data aggregation.

Voice Commands & Recognition

The app listens briefly to capture your spoken request or to identify a song you love. This audio processing happens locally on your device and is not transmitted, recorded, or stored.

Analytics & Connectivity

We connect only to your selected music service (e.g., Apple Music) to fulfill playback requests. No analytics services are enabled, and nothing beyond the commands you give is shared.

Device Permissions

Any permissions requested are strictly to access playback controls or the microphone during the moment you issue a command. There is no background monitoring.

Updates

Should anything change, we will update this policy and reflect the date above.
